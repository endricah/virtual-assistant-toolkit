# ✈️Travel Management SOP

## Objective

To ensure seamless, standardized, and effective travel coordination by maintaining a consistent process for travel research, booking, documentation, and itinerary management.

## Scope

This SOP covers travel planning, requirement gathering, transportation and accommodation research, booking coordination, itinerary creation, documentation management, and post-trip review.

# Travel Planning Workflow

## 1. Requirement Gathering

Before beginning travel arrangements:

- Confirm travel dates, destination, budget, and purpose of travel.
- Identify traveler preferences, including:
  - Preferred airlines or airline loyalty programs
  - Accommodation preferences
  - Hotel loyalty programs
  - Transportation requirements
  - Other specific travel considerations

Understanding preferences ensures recommendations align with the traveler’s expectations and established routines.

# 2. Research & Logistics

## Flight & Transportation Research

- Compare suitable flight or transportation options based on:
  - Schedule
  - Cost
  - Convenience
  - Traveler preferences

## Accommodation Research

- Compare accommodation options based on:
  - Location
  - Budget
  - Reviews
  - Required amenities
  - Existing hotel preferences or loyalty programs

## International Travel Verification

For international travel:

- Verify visa requirements
- Confirm entry requirements
- Check required travel documentation

# 3. Booking Process

- Proceed with bookings only after final confirmation of dates, budget, and preferred options.
- Ensure booking approval or confirmation is provided in writing before completing arrangements.
- Save all booking confirmations, invoices, and receipts in the appropriate Google Drive folder for future reference and expense tracking.

# 4. Master Travel Document Structure

Each travel plan should include:

## Trip Summary

- Travel dates
- Purpose of travel
- Emergency contact information

## Travel Details

- Flight numbers
- Departure and arrival times
- Transportation arrangements

## Accommodation Details

- Hotel or accommodation name
- Check-in and check-out times
- Address
- Confirmation numbers

## Preparation Checklist

Include essential preparation items:

- Passport or identification documents
- Contracts or business materials
- Samples or required items
- Other travel essentials

# Best Practices

## Centralization

All travel documents should be stored in the shared Google Drive project folder to ensure relevant stakeholders have real-time access.

## Communication

Send a pre-trip briefing to the traveler approximately 48 hours before departure, including:

- Final itinerary
- Important contact information
- Key travel reminders

## Flexibility Planning

Maintain alternative options where possible, such as:

- Backup accommodation
- Alternative transportation routes
- Contingency plans for cancellations or unexpected changes

# Post-Trip Process

## Expense Audit

- Collect and organize receipts.
- Consolidate expenses into an expense report for reimbursement or record keeping.

## Debrief & Improvement

Document any challenges, delays, or issues experienced during the trip and identify improvements for future travel planning.

# Tools Used

- Google Workspace for documentation and storage
- Google Drive for travel files and receipts
- Calendar tools for scheduling
- Email platforms for communication
- Travel booking platforms
