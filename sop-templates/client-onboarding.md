# Introduction 
*This SOP outlines the steps to onboarding a new client smoothly and professionally.*

##**1. Initial Contact**

- Send a welcome email
- Gather basic information (name, business, goals)

##**2. Schedule Discovery Call**

- Set up calender invite (calendly, google calendar)
- prepare a list of quetsions to understand the client's needs.

##**3. Contract and Payment**

  - Send contract for review and signature.
  - Confirm payment setup (invoice or latform)
 
##**4. Kickoff and Expectations**

    - Schedule the official kicckofff meeting.
    - Outline key milestones and communication expectations.

 ##**5. Onboarding Checklist**

 - List key documents and tools the client needs to share.

##**6. Follow-up**

- After the kickoff, set a follow-up call(e.g.,one weeek in) to check progress.
